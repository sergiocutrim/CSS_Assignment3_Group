A little bit about us..

Big Brothers Big Sisters of Canada (BBBSC) is a national charitable organization dedicated to helping children and youth reach their full potential through mentorship. Founded in 1913, BBBSC has grown into a network of over 100 local agencies that provide life-changing mentoring relationships to thousands of young people across Canada.

The organization operates under the belief that every child deserves to have someone who believes in them and provides support, guidance, and friendship. Through a variety of mentoring programs, BBBSC pairs adult volunteers (Big Brothers and Big Sisters) with children and youth (Littles) who may be facing adversities, such as poverty, family instability, or social isolation. These one-on-one relationships are designed to foster the Littles' confidence, resilience, and social skills, helping them navigate challenges and envision brighter futures.
