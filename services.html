FIND THE NEAREST AGENCY!

Becoming a mentor means guiding and supporting individuals in their personal growth. Your experience and advice can help shape a better future for others. Mentees and mentors connect and explore their local community together, spending around 6-8 hours a month on activities, events, or community resources they choose based on their interests. Each match has the flexibility to create their own schedule, discovering shared interests and new experiences along the way. Every match receives ongoing support from a BBBS professional caseworker who ensures they stay safe, stay committed, and achieve their individual goals. Mentees and mentors also have the option to meet weekly at a designated location, like a school, where they commit to a regular schedule and work towards shared goals. The program's duration may vary, with mentors receiving specialized training to effectively guide their mentees within specific site-based programs.
